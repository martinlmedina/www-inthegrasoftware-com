We are redesigning the full website for Inthegra Software (inthegrasoftware.com).

Goal:
Create premium enterprise-level website mockups and front-end page concepts for a company specialized in Oracle-native enterprise software, industry products, AI-powered solutions, and Oracle services.

Brand positioning:
- Oracle-Native / Enterprise Software
- Inthegra wants to become the leading Oracle services and enterprise software company in Latam within the next 3 years
- The website must feel corporate, trustworthy, modern, high-end, and execution-driven
- Visual style should follow the direction already defined in the Services pages: premium enterprise, similar to Oracle / Accenture, not startup playful, not generic SaaS template

Commercial priorities:
1. Generate leads
2. Strengthen enterprise brand positioning
3. Support sales process
Main CTA logic:
- Products and Solutions => "Agendar demo"
- Services => "Hablar con un experto"

SMEs and mid-market companies with 40+ employees that need a strategic technology partner and modern enterprise software to support growth.

Core differentiators:
- 23 years of industry know-how
- End-to-end approach: product + cloud + AI
- Fast implementation
- Native ERP + vertical integration
- AI both in software development process and in products

Design direction:
- Desktop-first premium mockups

Output expected for each page:

3. Copywriting guidance for hero, section titles, supporting paragraphs and CTA buttons
