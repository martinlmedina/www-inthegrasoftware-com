# Prompt premium — ERP / Business Suite Inthegra

Using the global website context and the attached Inthegra portfolio sources, generate the premium mockup concept for the ERP / Business Suite page of Inthegra Software.

## PAGE SCOPE
This page must position Inthegra ERP as an enterprise-grade management platform for:
- retail minorista
- retail mayorista
- empresas de servicios

It must also clearly communicate one of Inthegra’s strongest differentiators:
this ERP does not live in isolation. It integrates naturally with the rest of the Inthegra portfolio, especially:
- Credit & Financial / Credit Suite
- HealthCare / Health Suite
- WMS
- Supply Chain
- CRM
- Ecommerce
- BI / Analytics
- RRHH / People solutions

## IMPORTANT BUSINESS TRUTH TO RESPECT
Do not present the ERP as a generic low-end administrative system.
This is a robust Oracle-native business platform with modular architecture and vertical expansion capability.
It must feel credible for companies that need operational control, finance visibility, stock accuracy, logistics coordination, branch execution, and business scalability.

## GROUNDING FROM PROVIDED SOURCES
Use the attached sources as hard context and reflect these ideas faithfully:
- Inthegra ERP Suite is adaptable to empresas de servicios, financieras, salud, retail mayorista, retail minorista y manufactura.
- Core ERP modules include: compras, ventas, clientes, proveedores, finanzas, contabilidad, impuestos, comercial, stock, logística, punto de venta, reporting, BI, ecommerce, RRHH, seguridad y cash management.
- WMS is integrated with stock operations and improves depósitos, inventario visibility, picking and order preparation.
- Supply Chain supports clusters, demand visibility, assisted replenishment, assisted purchasing, automatic replenishment, stock minimums, equivalents and branch / distribution center logic.
- Credit Suite covers tarjetas de crédito no bancarizadas, préstamos, cobranzas, CRM clientes, CRM comercios, scoring, otorgamiento y cajas.
- Health Suite covers afiliaciones, autorizaciones online, auditorías, prestadores, liquidaciones and related admin-financial processes.
- CRM consumes ERP data and enables omnichannel commercial interaction.
- Ecommerce can be deeply connected with ERP stock, products and parametrization.
- Inthegra’s technology base is Oracle-native, deployed on OCI, using Oracle Database and Oracle APEX.

## PAGE OBJECTIVE
The page must help a prospect immediately understand:
1. Inthegra ERP is a serious operational backbone.
2. It serves both service companies and retail operations.
3. It scales from core administration to advanced operational execution.
4. It can extend into specialized industry products without forcing disconnected systems.
