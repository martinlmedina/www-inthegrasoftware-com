IMPORTANT CONTEXT (VERY IMPORTANT):
This is NOT a generic CRM.

Inthegra CRM has TWO CORE CAPABILITIES:
1. Sales CRM (lead, opportunity and commercial management)
2. Collections CRM (debt management, payment follow-up, and recovery workflows)

This dual approach is a key differentiator and MUST be clearly reflected.

The platform is designed for:
- Companies that need real operational control over sales AND collections
- Credit companies, lenders, retail financing, and service businesses
- Organizations that require tight integration between CRM, ERP and financial workflows

The CRM is part of a broader ecosystem:
- Integrated with ERP (Business Suite)
- Connected to Credit & Financial systems
- Can integrate with chatbot, automation and analytics
